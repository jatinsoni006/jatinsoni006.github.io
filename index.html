Our Team

MVRT is a student-run high school robotics team that participates in the annual FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC).

Events

Keep up with MVRT's events here!

FIRST

FIRST is an organization whose goal is to spread STEM values, and inspire and enable students to be innovative thinkers. Their programs also challenge students's teamworking and problem-solving abilities, giving them a "sneak peek" into the professional world.

Outreach

In addition to building and programming robots, MVRT actively reaches out to others by constantly giving back to the community, by spreading STEM and FIRST values.
